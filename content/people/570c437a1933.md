name: Zofia Stemplowska
bio: Zofia Stemplowska joined Oxford in September 2012 from Warwick where she was Associate Professor of Political Theory. Before that she was Lecturer in Political Philosophy at Reading and at Manchester and a Barbara McCoy Postdoctoral Fellow at Stanford University. She studied PPE at New College and completed her MPhil and DPhil at Nuffield College. She grew up in Warsaw.
