name: Brian Earp
bio: Brian Earp is a Research Associate with the Oxford Centre for Neuroethics and a Consultant working with the Institute for Science and Ethics at Oxford's Martin School. Serving as Editor-in-Chief of both the international Yale Philosophy Review and the Yale Review of Undergraduate Research in Psychology, Brian also conducted extensive experimental research in a number of areas, generally touching on unconscious or automatic mental processes. With Professor Julian Savulescu, Brian is authoring a book on the neuroenhancement of love and marriage, to be completed this year.
