boss tells you that from now on your department will be doing things differently.  To improve productivity there will be minor adjustments to the shift pattern, to the technology and to the organizational structure.  Do you welcome these changes?  No, then perhaps you are resistant to change?  Quassim Cassam is here to tell us whether that is a character flaw.
