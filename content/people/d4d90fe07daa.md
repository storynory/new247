name: Ole Martin Moen
bio: Ole Martin Moen is a philosopher at University of Oslo who works on how to think straight about thorny ethical issues. He is Principal Investigator of “What should not be bought and sold?”, a 4-year/$1 million project funded by the Research Council of Norway. He is also co-founder and chairman of Humanistskolen, a non-profit charter school in Oslo with 60 full-time students (age 13 to 16) that emphasises  philosophy and critical thinking.
