Do you ever get the feeling – perhaps whilst struggling to finish a project, complete an essay, do a deal, secure a promotion, campaign for a cause - that it’s all a waste of time – futile – that life is meaningless?   So is life meaningless?  The Israeli philosopher Iddo Landau has written a book on the meaning of life – and is here to reassure you that it’s not!
