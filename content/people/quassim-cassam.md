bio: |-
  Quassim Cassam is Professor of Philosophy at the University of Warwick. Cassam
  was born in Mombasa, Kenya and educated at Keble College, Oxford. He writes on self-knowledge,
  perception, epistemic vices and topics in Kantian epistemology. In 2016 he was awarded
  a Leadership Fellowship by the Arts and Humanities Research Council for an 18 month
  project on intellectual vices ('Vice Epistemology'). The project's main output will
  be a book called Vices of the Mind.
