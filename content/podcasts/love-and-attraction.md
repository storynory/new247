If you drink a couple of beers, you’ll notice various changes to your outlook on life.  You may, for example, find that a person you previously found unappealing has miraculously morphed into somebody considerably more attractive.  Anders Sandberg has been reflecting on the neurobiology of love and attraction – he wonders whether, in the future, we might consider popping love pills.
